About our school



Kakawate National High School is a Public school, diverse and inclusive institution committed to providing a high-quality
education to students from 7th grade through 12th grade. We believe that every child has the potential to succeed,
and we work hard to create a safe, supportive, and engaging learning environment that fosters academic growth
and personal development. Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects such as math,
science, English, and social studies, as well as a variety of elective courses, including foreign languages,
visual and performing arts, and technology. We have a team of experienced and dedicated teachers
who are passionate about their subjects and who work tirelessly to support the individual needs of each student.


In addition to academic coursework, Kakawate National High School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities,
including sports teams, music ensembles, drama clubs, and community service programs.
These activities provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, build self-confidence,
and explore their interests outside of the classroom.


Kakawate National High School is also committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for all students.
We have a team of counselors and support staff who work closely with students to address their social and emotional needs,
and we have strict policies in place to prevent bullying and harassment.


Our school has a strong record of academic achievement, and our graduates have gone on to attend some of the top colleges and universities in the country.
We are proud of our students' accomplishments and are dedicated to providing them with the tools and resources they need
to succeed in college and beyond. At Kakawate National High School, we believe that education is a partnership between the school,
the family, and the community. We welcome and value the involvement of parents, community members, and local organizations in our school's activities and events.
We believe that by working together, we can provide our students with the best possible education and prepare them for a bright future.
